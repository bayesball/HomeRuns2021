Datafile runs_scored_2021.csv
Description of variables
----------------------------------------------------
HALF.INNING - identification of half-inning
Outs.Inning - number of outs in half-inning
Runs.Inning - number of runs scored in half-inning
Team - batting team
Home - indication of home (1) or away (0)
Pitcher - Retrosheet code for pitcher
SP - number of runners in scoring position
R_SP - number of runners from scoring position who score
Estimate - estimate at the pitcher Runs ability
League - team league
